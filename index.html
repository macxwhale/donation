From Scarcity to Spiritual Abundance

In many rural communities across Kenya, a single, tattered Bible is often shared between multiple families. Children yearn to read the Word of God but often have to wait weeks for their turn.

Our mission is simple yet profound: to place a brand new, durable Bible in the hands of 1,000 children. By providing their own copy, we empower them to study, grow, and share their faith independently.

Your gift of KSh 750 provides one Bible and covers the cost of getting it safely into a child's hands.

Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of Bibles are being provided? +
We provide durable, hardback Bibles in both English and Swahili, depending on the child's preference and region.
How are children selected? +
We work with local community leaders and churches to identify children from low-income families who do not currently own a Bible.
Is my donation tax-deductible? +
As a fictional project for this demonstration, we are not a registered non-profit. In a real scenario, we would provide tax receipts through our partner NGOs.
